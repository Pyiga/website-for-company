FAQ

+

How much does it cost to join the Kiyinda Coffee Store program?

The cost varies depending on the specific services and support you require. For a precise estimate, please contact us directly. We will discuss your needs and provide a detailed quote based on your requirements.

+

How long does it take to receive training?

The duration of our training programs varies depending on the type of training. Typically, sessions range from a few days to several weeks. We will work with you to develop a schedule that fits your needs.

+

How can I associate with Kiyinda Coffee Store?

You can associate with Kiyinda Coffee Store by contacting us through our website or visiting our office in Kiyinda, Busimbi Sub-County, Mityana District. We will discuss your needs and provide the necessary support and guidance to get you started.

+

What support do you offer for coffee processing?

We offer comprehensive support for coffee processing, including training on modern processing techniques, quality control, and packaging. Our goal is to help you produce high-quality coffee that meets international standards.

+

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including checks, mobile money, and all major credit cards. Payment schedules can be arranged to suit your financial planning and budget.
